STAGES OF ARCHAEOLOGICAL CONSERVATION

Odisha State Archaeology follows a systematic and scientific approach to safeguard cultural heritage â€” from discovery to long-term preservation â€” integrating modern methods with traditional care.

01

Survey & Exploration

Identification and documentation of archaeological sites such as Asurgarh and Sisupalgarh, including river valleys like Prachi and Daya.

02

Protection & Notification

Legal protection under the Odisha Ancient Monuments Preservation Act, 1956. Public notification and objection review for declared monuments (222 protected sites).

03

Excavation

Scientific excavation at sites like Maraguda, Biratagada, Golbai, and Sankarjang to understand cultural sequences.

04

Conservation & Preservation

Reversible treatment methods, material retention, and continuous maintenance at heritage sites such as Konark and Puri.
