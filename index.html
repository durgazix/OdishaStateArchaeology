ODISHA STATE ARCHAEOLOGY

The land of Odisha and its Geographical settings has a distinct position in the archaeological map of India. Geomorphologically the state characterised byEastern Ghat mountain region, coastal plain, central river basins and Northen plateau. The river system and foothill of the state has rich in providing appropriate conditions forthe development human colonisation,which indicates the rich archaeological vestiges ranging from the Pleistocene to the recent periods. The archaeological heritage of Odisha deals with prehistoric, protohistoric, early historic, medieval and colonial antiquities. Archaeological remains of Odisha divided into religious and secular ideology. Odisha State Archaeology meant for preserve and promote Odisha's rich archaeological and historical heritage as well as identification and documentation of Archaeological site, remains forfuture posterity.
